I am a PhD student in the Theory Group at Columbia advised by Henry Yuen. Before that I was a graduate student at the Institute for Quantum Computing at the University of Waterloo, advised by John Watrous, and before that I was an undergraduate at Carnegie Mellon University. I study theoretical computer science, with a focus on quantum computation.

I am generally happy to talk about anything related to computer science, so if you have a question or idea please feel free to reach out to me!
